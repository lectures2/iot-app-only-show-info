IoT Security Best Practices

Welcome to our Internet of Things (IoT) security best practices guide! This app provides SMEs' employees and the general public with reference for general safeguards on IoT devices.

Topics:

  • Asset Protection

    This section covers best practices for protecting the assets of your IoT devices, including physical security, authentication, and encryption.

  • Third-Party Management

    This section focuses on the security considerations when working with third-party vendors or service providers for your IoT infrastructure.

  • Operation and Maintenance

    Here, you'll find best practices for the ongoing operation and maintenance of your IoT devices, including software updates, configuration management, and access control.

  • Monitoring and Incident Management

    This section covers strategies for monitoring your IoT devices, detecting and responding to security incidents, and incident management.

Additional Requirements:

This app can be used without an internet connection. Contents are fully downloaded upon app installation.
